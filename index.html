About Me

My name is Kieran McManus, a digital consultant and experienced web developer and designer. I specialize in working with Nonprofit organizations, small businesses, and Mom & Pop shops. I will work with you on a custom-tailored solution to get your business to where it needs to be in the digital space.

I am a husband, musician, tech enthusiast, and proud rescue dog owner. Outside of work I love to travel and spend time with those I love. My mission is to truly help your vision come to life, and will work within your budget to achieve your goals.

Featured Work

Loudoun Music Instruction

loudounmusic.com

Loudoun Music Instruction is a small business music instruction school in Lovettsville, Virginia. The site was constructed with a custom Wordpress theme, built from the ground up with PHP, Bootstrap, Javascript, jQuery, HTML & CSS, featuring a fully functional blog, event calendars, registration form, contact form, custom branding/design/photography, and all info necessary for any small business.

World Animal Protection

protecttheanimals.org

World Animal Protection is a global non-profit organization dedicated to improving the well-being of animals. This donation site was part of a TV ad campaign designed to drive monthly donations to help its mission. This was built using Blackbaud Luminate forms and I jumped into this existing project, learned the CMS and re-designed the page, fixed issues, and made everything mobile-responsive.

The Webster Apartments

websterapartments.org

The Webster Apartments is a non-profit organization in New York, New York, providing affordable and safe housing for young women in NYC. The site was built using a premium Wordpress theme, and heavily customized with HTML & CSS.

Lace & Angels

laceandangels.com

Lace & Angels is a boutique clothing line based in New York, NY. The temporary landing page was built with Squarespace, and integrated with a mailing list.
